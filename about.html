Who We Are

AgriTech - A short portfolio

Agritech is a leading specialist supplier of many innovative agricultural products, manfuctured by reputed international brands, in India.
Current Portfolio includes seeds and growbags, Farming equipments such as tractors, Tractors, Combine Harvesters, Rice Transplanters, Tractor Implements, Rotavator, Disc Plough, Disc Harrow, Fertilizer Spreader and Others (Dozers, Front Loader, Hay Balers and Sprayers), Fertilizers and Pesticides.
Agritech has long recognised the importance of providing the right product and support to its customers. We have technical-oriented and experienced professionals to advise, assist and deploy resource to achieve the optimum results and maximise their profits. We continually seek new innovative technologies and products from all over the world, which offers value to our customers.
Under the able direction and leadership of our management team, Agritech is a fast evolving brand that stands for customer service and people. Agritech has grown to be one of Kerala's most well-known supplier of high quality equipment and services by focussing to meet the ever changing needs of farmers, processors and customers.
